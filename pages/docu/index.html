{{ lib.smartvisu }} Inline-Documentation

This is the interactive and online {{ lib.smartvisu }} documentation. Here you will find the API to all widgets, some design modules and the icons and backgrounds. Use the widgets in your html-pages. The number is not limited. Some widgets require a unique identifier (id) while for most widgets this is optional. The gad/s or item/s may be used as often as you need them. Remember that each backend system may have it's own format for gad/s or item/s.
