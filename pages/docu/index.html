{{ lib.smartvisu }} Inline-Documentation

This is the interactive and online {{ lib.smartvisu }} documentation. Here you will find the API to all widgets, some design modules and the icons and backgrounds. Use the widgets in your html-pages, the number is not limited, but each widget needs it's own and unique identifier (id) on a page. The gad/s or item/s may be used as often as you need them. Mention that each driver has it's own format of the gad/s or item/s.
